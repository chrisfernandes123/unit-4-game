Crystal Collector

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
You win the game by matching your total score to the automatically generated number. You lose the game if your score goes above the random number.
There will always be at least 1 odd number to ensure a possible win scenario. Click the Start Game button to begin. Good luck!
Note: The hard game will use a higher number to guess and will have higher values for each crystal. The crystals will also swap positions each game.
